Trading Fee Tier:

ORME utilizes a trading fee tier system, rewarding community members for high volume trading. To put it simply, the more the user trades the lower their trade fees will be. Each users volume will be calculated according to the total amount traded equivalent to BTC at the time of the trade.

The users fee tier is determined by their total trading volume over a 30-day period. At 00:00 UTC each day, the users trading volume will be evaluated and their fees will automatically be updated within the hour.

Example:
The user buys 15 EOS for 1 LTC and at the time of the trade 1 EOS is equivalent to 0.001 BTC. This means 0.015 BTC will then be added to their 30-day trade volume total.
