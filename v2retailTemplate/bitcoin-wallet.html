How to set up a Bitcoin wallet

One of the most widely used Bitcoin wallets is Blockchain wallet, named after the technology Bitcoin is based on. The Bitcoin wallet is easy and quick to set up and provides all the necessary security features. Follow this guide to set up your own wallet.

Step 1: Visit the Blockchain wallet website https://www.blockchain.com/.

Be sure to bookmark the url, as there are many fraudulent mirror sites.

Step 2: Set up a wallet

To set up your wallet, fill in your email address, choose a strong password and agree to our Terms and Conditions and then select ‘Create my wallet’. You have successfully opened your Bitcoin wallet.

How to send and receive Bitcoin

Receive Bitcoin

Head over to your dashboard and select ‘Request’ to obtain your Bitcoin address.

You can make BTC withdrawals from the Veritex exchange by inputting your BTC address into the address box in the Veritex BTC withdraw pop-up.

Send Bitcoin

Head to your dashboard and select ‘Send’.

Enter the BTC destination address and amount of Bitcoin you’d like to send, then click ‘Continue’.

How to secure your account

Step 1: Confirm your personal email

After creating your wallet, you will receive a verification email to your inbox. In your email, click on “Yes, This is My Email” to confirm your details.

Step 2: Backing up your security phrase

Log in to your account and head over to the ‘Security Center’ tab in the upper right menu of the dashboard. Select ‘Backup Phrase’. This is the first step to securing your wallet credentials.

Print the ‘Recovery Sheet’ and safekeep your recovery phrase or write it down. Make sure to store this information in a safe place.
